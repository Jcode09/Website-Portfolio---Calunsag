PhotoBel Machine Vision

Info

The project "Image Editor", A working Software of the Programming task, can convert the inserted image into grayscale, binary image or black and white, Process thresholding with one Threshold(T) value and also with boundaries(limits) the T1 & T2,Do segmentation of the image, Display the Area of the image and the Area of the object, Display the Centroid of the Image and the Centroid of the Object by showing the values of its x & y coordinates and marking them, Marking the centroid, you must show where that coordinate is found. Whether you place a cross or a plus sign is depend on your style, Box the image and all the objects inside the image, Rotate the image to any Degree inputted by the user, Mirroring of the image, either Vertical or Horizontal Mirroring, Translate, shift or move the image to any (x, y) coordinates, Can Illustrate an RGB Histogram of a certain image, It can show the Binary Projection of an Image via Horizontal & Vertical Projection, Can do the different Convolution filters such as: a. Smoothing b. Gaussian Blur c. Sharpening d. Mean Removal e. Emboss Laplacian and other types of embossing f. Custom Filter (where you can input your kernel values) and has own Facial recognition.
